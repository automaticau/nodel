Nodel

A digital media control system for museums and galleries

Nodel is an open source gallery digital media control system built on Java and Python.

It uses a series of nodes that perform "actions" or respond to "events" to control various devices on a network, allowing simple, easy and quick management of digital media devices.

Nodel is typically used to control digital media in galleries, museums, corporate meeting rooms and digital signage.

In short, Nodel can manage any device that resides on a network.

Why Nodel?

  • It is simple to deploy, operate and maintain
  • It runs on any operating system
  • It runs on any browser including mobile devices
  • Best of all, it is open source and free
